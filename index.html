About Darkhan Juzz

Darkhan Juzz (1990–2023) — Kazakhstani indie pop artist, songwriter, sound producer and musician. In 2023 he was in Apple Music Top 100: Kazakhstan.

Important Events

  2. 2016 — First songs

  4. 2023 — Beu Awards 2023
  5. 2023 — Died in an accident in Thailand
